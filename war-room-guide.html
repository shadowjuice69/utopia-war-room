GETTING STARTED

Creating Your Account

1

Visit the War Room URL above. You'll see the login screen.

2

Tap SIGN UP, enter your email and a password, then tap CREATE ACCOUNT. Your account is created immediately — no email confirmation needed.

3

Log in with your email and password. The app remembers you on return visits.

Sign Out: Tap the red 🚪 SIGN OUT button in the top-right corner at any time.

Joining the Shared Kingdom

The War Room automatically detects which kingdom you belong to. Simply paste your province page (SoT) into the My Kingdom section — the app reads the kingdom number and connects you to the shared kingdom data instantly. Everyone in the same kingdom sees the same data.

🎯 TARGET PLANNER

The main view — your kingdom on the left, enemy kingdom on the right. Side-by-side comparison of all provinces with RNW (Relative NetWorth) automatically calculated.

Loading Data

MY KINGDOM Tap CSV to upload your kingdom CSV file, or Paste to paste directly from the Utopia kingdom overview page or a single province page (SoT).

ENEMY KD Same options — CSV or Paste. Multiple enemy kingdoms are supported. Each gets its own tab so you can switch between them.

Filters

✓ Attackable Shows only provinces within your attack range (0.567–1.6 RNW).

🔥 Fana Filter provinces with Fanaticism.

💉 BL Filter provinces with Blood Lust.

Province Quick View

The dropdown at the top of the page shows all your My Kingdom provinces. Select any one to instantly see their full stats — NW, Offense, Defense, BE, Mana, TPA, WPA, Stealth, Honor, MAP, and active spells.

🌊 WAVE PLANNER

Plan coordinated attack waves by pairing your attackers with enemy targets. Tracks offense available, defense to break, and leftover offense per assignment.

Assigning Attacks

1

In Target Planner, tap a My Kingdom province row — it turns yellow ⚡ (selected as attacker).

2

Tap an Enemy KD province row — they get paired. The attacker's OP auto-fills.

3

Keep the attacker selected and tap more enemy rows to assign multiple targets.

4

Tap the same enemy row again with the same attacker to add a chain hit (🔗×2, ×3 etc.).

5

Multiple different attackers hitting the same target also counts as a chain.

After the Attack

Tap 📋 Result on any wave row and paste the attack result from your Utopia news. The tool automatically extracts acres taken, OP sent, kills, and unit losses — updating both your attacker's remaining OP and the enemy's defense estimate.

Attacker Balance

Each attacker shows a live progress bar: how much OP they've sent vs how much they have left. Turns yellow when over 50% used, red when over 80%.

📋 INTEL STORAGE

Full stats card for every province — both My Kingdom and Enemy. Everything captured from CSV, SoT pastes, or kingdom overview pages is stored here.

Each province card shows: NW, Acres, Offense, Defense, BE, Mana, TPA, WPA, Stealth, Honor, MAP, active spells, Fana/BL status, and intel age.

Add Notes and set an Ops Status (pending, done, skip, priority) per province — these save automatically.

✨ OPS PLANNER

Tap the ✨ Ops button on any enemy province row to open the ops assignment panel.

Three tabs:

✨ Spells — All offensive spells. Shows which of your My Kingdom provinces can cast each spell based on their race/personality combo. Tap a province button to assign them to that spell.

🗡 Sabotage — All thievery sabotage operations. Rogue-only ops only show Rogue provinces, etc.

🕵 Espionage — Intel operations. All provinces can do these.

📋 Assigned — Summary of all ops assigned to this target, with remove buttons.

Tip: The Ops button shows a count badge (✨3) when ops are assigned to that province so you can see at a glance which targets have plans.

📜 AGE CHANGES

Track race, personality, unit, and spell changes between ages.

1

Tap Load Current Age and paste the current age changes post — supports both the in-game wiki page format and the forum post format. Auto-detected.

2

Tap Load Next Age and paste the next age changes when they're announced.

3

The diff view automatically highlights what changed: BUFF, NERF, NEW, REMOVED.

Filter by: All, Changed Only, Races, Personalities, or Spells.

📰 KINGDOM NEWS

Track land taken and lost across all provinces, split by In War vs Out of War.

1

Enter your kingdom number (e.g. 4:9) in the KD # field.

2

Tap Paste News and paste your kingdom news page.

3

The tool automatically detects the war declaration date and splits events into ⚔ In War and 🕊 Out of War tabs.

What it tracks:

Per province and per kingdom: Acres Taken, Acres Lost, Net land change, Kills, and total attack count. My Kingdom net is shown in the summary bar at the top. Enemy kingdoms are highlighted separately from other kingdoms.

War start and end dates (Utopia calendar dates) are shown at the top of the In War tab.

Tip: You can paste news multiple times — new events are merged in without duplicating existing ones.

🔄 SHARED KINGDOM DATA

The War Room syncs data across all kingdom members automatically.

When you paste your province page (SoT), the app reads your kingdom number and joins the shared kingdom pool. Everyone else in the same kingdom sees your update in real time — no manual sharing needed.

Shared between all members: My Kingdom provinces, wave assignments, kingdom news.

Personal per member: Enemy KD intel, ops assignments, notes.

📡 AUTO-INTEL SETUP

The War Room can automatically receive and update your province data every time you visit pages in Utopia. This means your stats, science, buildings, and military update live — no CSV needed.

What it does

When you visit any of these pages in Utopia, your data is automatically sent to the War Room:

  • Throne Page — NW, acres, offense, defense, BE, mana%, stealth%, gold, food, runes
  • Science Page — TPA, WPA, all science levels
  • Military Page — troop counts, off/def points
  • Survey Page — building breakdown
  • Kingdom News — attack reports and events

  3. When asked "Enter where to send intel", paste this URL:
    https://utopia-ops-bot-production.up.railway.app/intel
  4. When asked for a password/key, enter:
    NikkoAce
  5. Click OK — you're done!

After setup

Just play Utopia normally. Every time you visit your throne page, science, military, or survey, your War Room updates automatically within 2 minutes. No copy-pasting, no CSV needed.

The 📡 Province Intel tab in the Intel Command Center shows everything received — throne stats, science levels, troop counts, and building percentages all in one place.

TIPS & SHORTCUTS

• CSV upload — accepts any file type on Android (not just .csv). Rename your export if needed.

• Province page paste — paste a single SoT to update one province without replacing the whole kingdom.

• Intel age — shown in each row. Green = fresh, yellow = hours old, red = days old. Paste a new SoT to refresh.

• Export/Import — use the Export button in the header to save a full backup of all your data as a JSON file. Import it back at any time.

• Multiple enemy kingdoms — each enemy kingdom gets its own tab. Switch between them by tapping the tab in the Enemy KD section.

• Auto-login — the app remembers your session so you don't need to log in every time. Tap 🚪 SIGN OUT to switch accounts.
